Meet Rohit

A middle-class dreamer trying to build a future through effort, discipline, and absolute consistency.

No Talent

He wasn't born a genius. Everything he achieved had to be carved with direct raw labor and hours of intense concentration.

Middle Class

Bound by financial anxiety and expectations. To fail means losing everything; resources are scarce, risk is terrifying.

No Support

Fighting a solitary battle in the dark. No mentors, no backing. Just backhanded comments and skeptical looks.

Lots Of Pain

Wounds from failures, public embarrassments, and promises broken. A fuel that burns slowly, deep inside.

Overthinking

Mind constantly racing at 3 AM. Questioning every move, fearing the future, analyzing every word.

100% Efforts

The equalizer. When variables are skewed and luck is absent, his sheer will, discipline, and work ethic shift the balance.
